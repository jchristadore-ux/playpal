Frequently asked questions

How do I share a round with my group?

Start a round, then tap the sync code in the top bar (or the QR button). Friends tap JOIN ROUND on their home screen and enter the code, or scan the QR with their camera.

Scores aren't syncing between phones.

Both phones need an internet connection and must be in the same round code. Scores entered offline are saved on the device and sync the next time the app is online. If a round looks stuck, exit the round (EXIT tab) and rejoin with the same code — nothing is lost.

How do payouts work?

Each game format (Wolf, Nassau, Skins, …) computes winnings from the stake you set during round setup. The Results screen shows who owes whom, with optional Venmo links if players added their handles.

How do I add a course that isn't listed?

On the course selection step, choose ADD A COURSE MANUALLY and enter par, yardage, and handicap for each hole. The course is saved to your library and synced to your group.

How do I delete my data?

Player profiles can be deleted in the app (open the profile → DELETE). To erase everything associated with you from the sync database, email jchristadore@gmail.com — see the Privacy Policy.

Does PlayPal handle real money?

No. PlayPal only does the math. Settling up is between you and your group.

Reporting a bug

Email a description of what happened, what you expected, and your device/browser to jchristadore@gmail.com. Screenshots help a lot.
