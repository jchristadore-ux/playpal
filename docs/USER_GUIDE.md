# PlayPal User Guide

Everything your foursome needs to set up, score, and settle a round.

## Starting a round

**HOME → START NEW ROUND**, then three steps:

1. **Players** — pick 2–6 from your saved profiles.
2. **Course** — search by name or town. ⭐ star courses you play often
   (they pin to the top), and your last 10 courses appear under
   **Recently Played**. Pick your **tee box** if the course has more than
   one (this drives yardages *and* course handicaps), choose a
   **1st or 10th tee start**, and flip on **Track Round Stats** if you want
   fairways/greens/penalties/short-game numbers for the day.
3. **Games & Formats** — add any mix of:
   * **Match Formats** (new) — stroke play, match play, four ball, best
     ball, scrambles, alternate shot, Chapman, shamble, stableford, quota,
     skins, Nassau, sixes, and more. Gross or net, your call.
   * **Money Games** — the classic PlayPal side games with automatic
     payouts (Wolf, Nassau, Skins, Pass the Money, Bingo Bango Bongo,
     Tee Ball, Markey Match, Stableford).

### Setting up a match format

Tap **+ ADD GAME**, pick a format, and PlayPal pre-fills everything:

* **Teams** are auto-balanced by handicap (tap a letter to move someone,
  or ⚖️ AUTO-BALANCE to redo it).
* **NET/GROSS** toggle, with the recommended **handicap allowance**
  pre-filled per format (e.g. 90% for four-ball, 35/15 blend for a
  2-person scramble). Adjust if your group plays different numbers.
* **Strokes off low ball** is on by default for match-style games — the
  best player plays at scratch and everyone else gets the difference.
* **Adjust handicaps for this game** lets you override anyone's index for
  just this round (sandbaggers beware).

A green **✓ Ready to play** means the game is set.

## Handicaps

Each profile stores a **Handicap Index**. On every round PlayPal computes a
true **Course Handicap** from the tee you chose
(Index × Slope ÷ 113 + Rating − Par), applies the game's allowance, and
spreads strokes across holes by stroke index — including 9-hole rounds,
plus handicaps, and second strokes past 18.

The **↻ SYNC** button next to the index (player profile) connects to a
handicap service when one is configured; until then it simply tells you so
and the number stays editable by hand.

## Scoring

Scoring works exactly as before — big +/− steppers, tap the number for the
keypad, putt buttons, hole dots to jump around, everything live-synced to
your group via the room code/QR.

New on the score screen:

* **Scramble-style games**: enter the *team* score on any one teammate's
  card — PlayPal figures out the rest.
* **Stat tracking rounds** add FIR / GIR buttons plus **PEN** (penalty
  strokes), **SAND** (sand save ✓/✗) and **U&D** (up-and-down ✓/✗) on each
  player card.
* **GAME TRACKERS** drawer shows live standings for every game you set up —
  "Team A 2 UP thru 7 · dormie", skins carrying, stableford points, quota
  pace, all updating as scores land.
* 9-hole courses just work — the round ends after 9.

## After the round

**FINISH ROUND** takes you to results:

* **GAME RESULTS** — final standings and the winner for every match format
  ("Bird & Shark win 3&2", "Nassau splits 1–1", …).
* **NET SCORES** — gross and net totals side by side once anyone has a
  handicap.
* **📤 SHARE** — a clean text scorecard (standings, game results, money)
  via your phone's share sheet, or straight to the clipboard.
* **⬇️ EXPORT CSV** — the full hole-by-hole card for spreadsheets.
* Payouts and Venmo settlement for money games, exactly as before.

## Stats & trends (📈 STATS tab)

Pick a player to see their story across every saved round on this device:

* Scoring average, best round, fairways %, greens %, putts per round.
* A scoring **trend chart** of the last 12 full rounds.
* **Score distribution** (eagles → triples), par-3/4/5 averages, sand save
  and up-and-down rates, lifetime penalties.
* **Personal bests** — low round, best nine, most birdies, fewest putts,
  longest drive/putt (when tracked), most-played course.
* **Compare rounds** — pick any two rounds and see them hole by hole.

## Profiles

Tap any player on Home to edit their profile: handicap index, GHIN number,
Venmo, **preferred tees**, **righty/lefty**, and **home course** — plus a
shortcut to their career stats.

## Picking up where you left off

Leave mid-round (or your phone dies)? The Home screen shows a
**Round In Progress** card — tap **RESUME →** and you're back on the tee
you left. Finished rounds live under **Recent Rounds**, and completed
scorecards stay on the device for the Stats tab even when you're offline.
