Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026 · Applies to the PlayPal app and website.

What PlayPal is

PlayPal is a golf scoring companion built for a private group of golf friends. It is not an advertising-supported product, it does not sell data, and it contains no third-party trackers or analytics.

Information the app stores

  • Player profiles you create: name, initials, handicap index, GHIN number, email address, and Venmo handle. You choose what to enter; every field except name is optional.
  • Round data: golf scores, putts, game formats, stakes, and computed payouts.
  • Custom courses you add: course name, location, par/yardage/handicap values.
  • A random device identifier stored on your device, used only to avoid echoing your own live-score updates back to you.

Where it is stored

  • On your device (browser local storage) so the app works offline.
  • In Google Firebase (Firestore and Realtime Database, operated by Google LLC) so rounds sync between the devices in your group. Firebase's own privacy practices are described at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data synced to Firebase is shared with the other players in your PlayPal group — that is the point of the sync feature. Anyone with a round's share code can view that round's scores.

What PlayPal does not do

  • No advertising, no ad identifiers, no cross-app tracking.
  • No analytics or behavioral profiling.
  • No sale or sharing of personal information with third parties beyond the Firebase infrastructure described above.
  • No collection of location, contacts, photos, or health data.

Account & sign-in

PlayPal uses anonymous Firebase authentication — there is no username or password, and no account is created in your name.

Deleting your data

You can remove a player profile at any time inside the app (open the profile and tap Delete), which removes it from the synced database. To request deletion of all data associated with you — profiles, scores, and round history — email jchristadore@gmail.com and it will be removed within 30 days. Clearing the app's website data in your browser/device settings removes everything stored locally.

Data retention

Round history is kept so your group can review past rounds and trips. It remains until you delete it or request deletion as described above.

Children

PlayPal is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
