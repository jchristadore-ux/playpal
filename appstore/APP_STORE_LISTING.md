## Description
```
PlayPal is the scorekeeper your golf group has been arguing without.

Pick your players, pick your games, and score the round once — PlayPal keeps
every side game straight and settles the math before you reach the parking lot.

EIGHT GAMES, ONE SCORECARD
• Wolf — rotating wolf, partners or lone-wolf doubles
• Nassau — front, back, and overall, 1v1 or 2v2, with presses
• Skins — carryovers handled automatically
• Stableford — 5/3/2/1 points without the mental math
• Pass the Money — the chip moves, PlayPal watches it
• Bingo Bango Bongo — three points a hole, zero disputes
• Tee Ball — longest drive in play takes the point
• Markey Match — team match play with auto-presses

LIVE SYNC FOR THE WHOLE GROUP
Share a 6-character code or QR and every phone in the group sees scores update
in real time. One person scores, everyone watches the carnage.

PAYOUTS, DONE
Set the stakes during setup and the results screen shows exactly who owes
whom — with optional Venmo links to settle up. PlayPal never touches money;
it just ends the math debate.

GOLF TRIPS
Group rounds into trips and get a running leaderboard, earnings table,
birdie counts, and trip awards.

BUILT FOR THE COURSE
Works offline when the back nine loses signal. Handicaps and pops applied
per hole. Custom courses with full scorecard entry. No ads, no tracking,
no account required.
```
